Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the HUMMI application ("the App").

TL;DR: HUMMI is designed with strict privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All audio processing happens 100% on your device.

1. Data Collection and Usage

HUMMI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, user identifiers, or usage analytics to our servers or any third parties.

2. Microphone and Audio Data

The App requires access to your device's microphone to record audio for enhancement. All audio recording and processing happens entirely locally on your iPhone or iPad.

Your audio data never leaves your device. We do not upload your recordings to any cloud services, servers, or third-party APIs. There are no exceptions.

3. Third-Party Services

HUMMI does not integrate with any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising frameworks. Your usage of the app remains completely private and anonymous.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact the developer via the App Store support link or GitHub repository.
